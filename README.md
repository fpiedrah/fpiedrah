Before starting my Ph.D. at Brown, I served as an engineering lead at [Rappi](https://about.rappi.com/), where I worked on a range of machine learning and optimization problems. My work included forecasting orders for better fleet allocation, estimating delivery times, generating routes, and matching couriers with orders.

I'm excited by opportunities to contribute new ideas at the intersection of machine learning, reinforcement learning, and real-world decision-making systems.
